Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application PandaBeFree (hereinafter referred to as "App"), an open-source 3D printer dashboard for Bambu Lab printers, released under the MIT License.

In brief: We protect your privacy. The App operates entirely on your local network. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers. All information stays on your device.

3. Collection and Storage of Personal Data

a) Local Storage

Your printer connection details (IP address, access code) and cached printer state are stored exclusively on your device using local storage and shared UserDefaults (for widget functionality). We do not have access to this data. If you delete the App, this local data will also be removed.

b) Local Network Communication (MQTT)

The App connects directly to your Bambu Lab printer via MQTT over your local network (LAN). This communication happens entirely within your home network and does not pass through any external servers. The connection uses TLS encryption (port 8883) for secure communication between the App and your printer.

c) Camera Stream

If you use the camera preview feature, the video stream is received directly from your printer over your local network. No camera data is transmitted to us or any third party.

d) Home Screen Widgets

The App provides Home Screen widgets that display printer status, camera snapshots, and filament information. Widget data is cached locally on your device using a shared app group container. Widgets may briefly connect to your printer on your local network to refresh data.

e) App Store

When downloading the App, the App Store operator (Apple) collects data such as username and customer number. This data is not made available to us; we are not responsible for this data collection.

4. No Analytics, No Tracking, No Ads

We do not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or tracking frameworks. We do not display advertisements and do not share any data with advertising networks or other third parties.

5. No Server-Side Components

The App has no backend server, no cloud services, and no external API connections. All functionality operates entirely between the App on your device and your printer on your local network.

6. Permissions

The App requires access to your local network to communicate with your Bambu Lab printer. No other special permissions (camera, contacts, microphone, location) are requested.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, restrict, or delete your data. Since we do not maintain user accounts or collect any data externally, you can delete all your local data at any time by uninstalling the App.

8. Third-Party Dependencies

The App uses open-source libraries (such as CocoaMQTT for printer communication). These libraries operate locally and do not transmit data to external services. As the source code is publicly available, you can verify this independently.
